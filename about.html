* Gameplay:
* Once the game loads, you will see Koro Sensei popping up randomly on the screen.
* Your task is to quickly click/tap on Koro Sensei whenever he appears to prevent him from destroying Earth.
* Each successful click/tap on Koro Sensei earns you points and keeps the planet safe.
* Be careful not to click/tap on any other characters or objects, as this may result in penalties.

* Scoring:
* Points are awarded for each successful click/tap on Koro Sensei.

* End of Game:
* The game ends when you click the earth.
* After the game ends, your final score is displayed.
* Retry or Restart:
* If you wish to play again, simply refresh the page or restart the game.

Tips:
* Keep your cursor or finger ready to click/tap as soon as you spot Koro Sensei.
* Maintain focus and react quickly to his appearances to maximize your score.

Inspirations: Whac-A-Mole & Assasination Classroom
